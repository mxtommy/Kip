Start editing dashboards

Dashboards are "Locked" by default. This is to prevent accidental touch screens interaction from changing anything. To edit, add, remove and configure widgets in dashboards, you first need to access the Actions menu by swiping left and hit the edit dashboard button in the upper right hand corner. Go Ahead try it now!

Once you are editing a dashboard, you can:

  1. Touch and Drag to move widgets
  2. Drag the corners and sides to resize widgets
  3. Double Tap on a widget to configure it
  4. Long Press on a widget to access additional options such as delete amd duplicate
